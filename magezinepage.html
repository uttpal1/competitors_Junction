As we all know the importance of magazines for the preparation for the UPSC and STATE PCS.Number of magazines are available in the market but we have to focus on only few of them.Magazines contains different articles written by different authors on different subjects.It provides wide range of idea and thought which is helpful for Mains Examination and Interview.Do not get confuse, here are the list of some of the best magazines .

Best Magazine for UPSC Preparation

  1. Yojna
  2. Kurushetra
  3. Economic and Political Weekly
  4. Down to Earth

YOJNA

December 23, 2013 by Jacob

Yojna is one of the most important monthly magazine for the IAS candidate.It is published by the I&B ministry, Government of India. It available in 13 languages-English, Hindi, Assamese, Telugu, Gujarati, Bengali, Urdu, Punjabi, Marathi, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam and Oriya.Many successfull candidate has described yojna as the most important tool for the exam.It covers important topics like federalism, governance, budget, agriculture, etc.Every year one or two essays can be seen in the UPSC Main examination directly from Yojana magazine.Since it is a government magazine, it gives a balanced opinion -both pros and cons of any topic which are essential for Civil Services Examination, especially for Main and Interview preparation. The Yojana is helpful for General Studies Paper II and Paper III and Essay paper.

KURUSHETRA

December 23, 2013 by Jacob

It is also a monthly magazine which deals with Rural Development.It is published by the Ministry of Rural Development,Government of India.It discuss with the various issues related to agriculture and rural development.It discuss about various schemes introduced by the government.As we know UPSC is now focusing more on such topic so reading ths magazine will be helpful for Prelims and Mains Exam.

ECONOMIC AND POLITICAL WEEKLY

December 23, 2013 by Jacob

Economic and Political Weekly is most famous and reputed journal for social science.It publishes an analysis of contemporary affairs along with academic papers in social science.It is a multidisciplinary publication covring socialogy,history,environmental and political science.Articles are written by well-known activists and commentators.

DOWN TO EARTH

December 23, 2013 by Jacob

It is fortnightly magazine.It focus on environmental issues and awarness.It is useful for geography optional students.
